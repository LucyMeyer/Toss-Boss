About Us

Toss Boss is a 4-person arcade game stall offering fun, interactive challenges where players toss balls into colour-coded cups for points. Win enough points and score a delicious chocolate chip cookie!

How It Works

  • 🎯 Aim and toss balls into cups
  • 🟢 Each colour represents a different point value
  • 🍪 Score enough points to win cookies
  • 🎉 Primary students have easier prize criteria!
